OVERVIEW

Mario Kart Midnight 2 is a custom track distribution created by Mayro. This is the sequel to Mario Kart Midnight.
It aims to be a celebration of custom tracks, featuring the "best" tracks released between December 2022 and December 2023.
Every track released during this time period was featured in monthly public polls, which were voted on by custom track enthusiasts.
Every track that averaged a score of four or above out of five was guaranteed a spot in the distribution.
Select tracks that scored 3.5 out of five or higher were also included, with the rest being planned for future updates.
