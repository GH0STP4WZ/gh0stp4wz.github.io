Insane Kart Wi-Fi Connection

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected when you connect to Insane Kart Wi-Fi Connection (IKWFC), how it is used, and how it is handled. By using IKWFC, you agree to this policy.

2. Data We Collect

  • Console Identifier
  • IP Address
  • User IDs (Friend Codes)
  • Last known usernames associated with those User IDs

3. Use of Collected Data

The collected data may be used to operate the service, identify users, and investigate or enforce Terms of Service violations. Data is not sold or shared with third parties.

4. Enforcement Actions

If a Terms of Service violation is confirmed:

  • The IP address may be blacklisted
  • The Console Identifier may be rejected
  • All User IDs linked to the IP address and Console Identifier may be cleared

5. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact IKWFC staff through our Discord server.

Thank you for being part of the IKWFC community.
