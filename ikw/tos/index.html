Penalty: Immediate permanent ban without warning.

Penalty: Repeat offenses may lead to a permanent ban.

2.5 Inappropriate Mii Names (Mild Language)

Penalty: Temporary suspension ranging from 10 minutes to 30 minutes, at moderator discretion.

2.6 Offensive or Political Mii Names

Penalties:
First offense: 7-day ban
Second offense: 30-day ban

2.7 Impersonation of Public Figures

Penalty: Repeat offenses may result in a permanent ban.

Policy: Continued participation with unplayable lag may result in removal until resolved.
