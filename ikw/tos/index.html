1. General Conduct Guidelines

All players are expected to follow good sportsmanship, show respect to others, and adhere to fair gameplay principles. Most rules align with those enforced on the Retro Wi-Fi Connection (RWFC), with additional policies outlined below.

2. Prohibited Activities

The following actions are strictly prohibited on IKWFC and may result in disciplinary action, including temporary or permanent bans:

2.1 Cheating and Game Manipulation

  • Mass item hacks (e.g., infinite shocks or stars)
  • Speed modifications (minor or major)
  • Manipulation of item probability
  • Any other unauthorized advantages
Penalty: Immediate permanent ban without warning.

2.2 Server Manipulation

  • Starting other players' friend rooms without permission
  • Forcefully removing users from sessions without legitimate authority
Penalties:
First offense: 30-day ban
Subsequent offenses: Permanent ban
Ban evasion attempts: Permanent ban applied to all associated profiles

2.3 Trolling and Disruptive Gameplay

Malicious gameplay behavior such as targeted attacks, trolling, or intentionally ruining the experience for others is not tolerated.

Penalty: Repeat offenses may lead to a permanent ban.

2.4 Inappropriate Mii Names (Mild Language)

Penalty: Temporary suspension ranging from 10 minutes to 30 minutes, at moderator discretion.

2.5 Offensive or Political Mii Names

Penalties:
First offense: 7-day ban
Second offense: 30-day ban
Third offense: Permanent ban

2.6 Impersonation of Public Figures

Penalty: Repeat offenses may result in a permanent ban.

2.7 Emulator Lag

Policy: Continued participation with unplayable lag may result in removal until resolved.

2.8 Track Mismatch (Wrong Track Errors)

Penalties:
Accidental installation error: Up to 10-minute ban
Intentional misuse: Permanent ban

3. Enforcement and Moderator Discretion

Moderators reserve the right to apply or escalate punishments at their discretion based on context, severity, intent, and history of infractions. All decisions are made to protect the integrity of the server and ensure a fair environment.

Note: If there is definitive proof of a violation, we reserve the right to deny appeals without further discussion.
